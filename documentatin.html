What are we trying to solve?

The goal of ARC is to solve the growing problem of isolation within communities. Built for the purpose of encouraging/incentivizing increased social interaction and community contribution through team-building games and experiences.

Device Requirements

Smartphones mainly since it is going to be a mobile base app. Could possible some how make an online leaderboard website thing

System Requirements

Web service of some sort. Figuring out AR stuff. Data Storage, GPS aspects. Possible AI implementation. All zones and in-game environments will be based around and centered in public/safe/populated areas only, to increase their safety/decrease our liability.
